Get residents' or visitors' parking permits:step 4 of 5

Visitors' permits

  • You can apply for up to 100 visitors' permits per year.
  • You do not need to own a car to apply for visitors' permits.
  • You do not have to apply for all of your visitors' permits now. You can apply for more permits, at any time during the year.
  • Visitors' permits are printed in books of scratch cards, each permit is valid for one days parking.
