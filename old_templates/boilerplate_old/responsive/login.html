Make a freedom of information (FOI) request

You can ask to see any information we hold on paper or as computer files. The Freedom of Information Act (FOIA) 2000 gives you this right.

Before you start

  • Check if we have already published the information
  • To find out what personal information we hold about you make a data protection request

Our response

We'll contact you in three working days to let you know how long it's going to take us to respond.

The law requires that we send FOI information within 20 working days of your request.

Other formats

If you'd like to receive the information in another format, for example, braille or another language, please let us know when you fill in the form.
