If you go to the next step you give us permission to check your identity and address details with a company called Experian.

This means you do not have to send us any documents like a passport or driving license, so you get your bus pass sooner.

This won't affect your credit record, but the check will be recorded. Experian will check your details with other information they hold. They won't keep your information you entered on this form or give it to anyone else.

If you don't want us to check your details with Experian, stop now and do not click next. You must apply with the older person's bus pass paper form
